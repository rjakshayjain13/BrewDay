About Brew Day!

Home brewing, the process of producing beer on a small scale for personal purposes, is an activity that receives growing attention among beer enthusiasts. Every home brewer owns a set of brewing equipment (kettles, fermenters, pipes, etc.) with a certain maximum brewing capacity, the number of litres the equipment is able to handle in a single "batch". Brewing also requires ingredients, whose actual amounts vary from recipe to recipe; these include various kinds of malts, hops, yeasts and sugars (and of course, water). Brewers like to maintain a log their recipes for future reference, and maintain an updated list of available ingredients, for shopping before the next brew.

Brew Day! is an application that allows home brewers to maintain an organized database of their beer recipes. The application allows users to create, store and modify recipes, and later on delete them, if the user wishes to do so. The application is intended for "all-grain" brewers only, and thus all recipes are for this kind of brews (the "extract" brews are not supported).
